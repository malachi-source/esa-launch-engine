# SOP Filming Ads

**Instructions for Filming Ads:**

  - Each video should be 45-90 seconds
  - Record in selfie style walking around house or studio (The more organic, the better)
  - Video's should be shot in Reel/Story format (Vertical)
  - Speak directly to ONE person, not the whole audience
  - Record each video in ONE take if possible, if not try to do it in 2 takes (Body + CTA)
  - Be VERY direct with the BOLD statements and HOOKS
  - Feel the emotion in every word you speak (the more they feel like you understand them, the better results we'll get)
  - You can download a teleprompter app on your phone if you need to
  - Change any words to fit your personality and tone
