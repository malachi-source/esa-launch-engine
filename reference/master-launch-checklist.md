# Paid Ads and SMS/Emails

**Paid Ads and SMS/Emails**

**Paid Ads System Launch**

  - **Client Fills Out Marketing Survey**
  - **Build the "Ultimate Target Audience Sheet"**
  - **ESA Sends to Client for Approval**
  - Write 5 Persuasive Video Ad Scripts For Event (Hook, Body, CTA)
  - Write 5 High-Converting Ad Copy for Meta Ads
  - Write VSL (if part of the campaign)
  - Send Ad Scripts, Ad Copy, and to VSL Client for Feedback and Approval
  - Send Client Loom Walking Them Through Filming Process
  - Client Records Videos
  - Client Uploads Raw Footage to Google Drive & Notifies ESA In Slack
  - Send Footage + Brand Colors to Editor to Add Captions, Trim & Polish
  - Send Edited Ads & VSL to Client
  - Get Approval For Edited Ads and VSL from client and revise if needed
  - **Client Add Payment Method to Ad Account (Loom Here)**
  - Install Pixel + Create Custom Events (Opt-In Lead + Purchase)
  - Create 5 Cold Interest-Based Audiences in Meta
  - Upload GHL Leads as a Custom Audience on Meta
  - Create 17 Warm Retargeting Audiences in Meta Ads (Social Media + Website Visitors)
  - Launch 3 Meta Campaigns:
      - FB Form Lead Campaign
          - Build Form and Map inside GHL with opt-in automations
      - Purchase Conversion Campaign to Purchase Page
      - **Lead Conversion Campaign to Registration Page**
