# Facebook Lead Form — working notes

## What this step does
Writes complete, high-converting Facebook lead form copy for live events, webinars, and workshops — qualifying the right audience, creating urgency, and maximizing completed registrations.

## Inputs it needs (from the onboarding form / earlier steps)
- Event Details (name, type, date & time, format, host/speaker & credentials)
- Offer Details (main topic, target audience, primary pain point, transformation/outcome, price)
- Qualification Criteria (qualified vs. not qualified, required level/stage)
- Goals (what attendees should be excited about, action after registering)

## Output it produces
Full FB lead form package: form name, headline (3 options, PRIMARY marked), description, qualification question(s) with multiple-choice options, contact info question + required/optional fields, and ending/confirmation screen (headline, body copy, CTA button text + link).
