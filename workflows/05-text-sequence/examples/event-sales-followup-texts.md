# Event Sales Follow-Up Texts (swipe)

> Source: ESA "Event Sales Follow-Up Texts". Universal, fill-in-the-blank closing/
> follow-up SMS for getting registrants and prospects to commit. Use {Event Name},
> {specific outcome}, {big problem}, {your goal}, {desired result} as merge tokens.
> Also useful for 07-text-reengagement.

## Example: Event For Land Investors Looking to Scale With A Sales Team

**Week 1**
- Day 1: If I could help you close 2 extra deals next month without more leads, would you want to know how?
- Day 3: On a scale of 1-10, how serious are you about improving your close rate right now?
- Day 5: If you knew you were leaving $20K+ a month on the table, would you want to fix it?

**Week 2**
- Day 8: Are you leaning more towards "let's go" or "still deciding"?
- Day 10: If I gave you a script that gets past "I'll think about it," would you use it?
- Day 12: Are you in learning mode or action mode right now?

**Week 3**
- Day 15: If your team could close without you, what would that free you up to do?
- Day 18: Would you say you're closer to "yes," "not yet," or "no" on joining us?
- Day 20: If I could help you increase your close rate in the next 30 days, would you make the time?

**Week 4**
- Day 23: If you had to decide today, would it be yes or no?
- Day 25: If you had the same follow-up system as the top 1% of closers, would you use it?
- Day 27: Are you waiting for more info, or ready to commit?
- Day 29: If you could fix your biggest sales bottleneck this week, would you?
- Day 30: Is there anything stopping you from saying yes today?
