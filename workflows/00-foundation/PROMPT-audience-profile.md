## CRITICAL INSTRUCTIONS FOR CLAUDE

WHEN CREATING THIS PROFILE:

1.  RUTHLESSLY PRIORITIZE: Only include insights that directly impact copywriting. If it doesn't help write better ads/emails, cut it.
2.  ENFORCE 12-PAGE MAXIMUM: If you're exceeding 12 pages, consolidate sections. Quality over quantity.
3.  USE THEIR EXACT LANGUAGE: Quote them verbatim as much as possible. Their words = your best copy.
4.  FOCUS ON COPY OUTPUTS: Every section should produce ready-to-use copy elements (hooks, subject lines, objection handlers, etc.).
5.  MINE FOR SPECIFICS: Generic = useless. "They're frustrated with marketing" is worthless. "They're checking their dashboard 20 times a day and seeing 2 ticket sales when they need 200" is gold.
6.  EXTRACT THE PSYCHOLOGY: Why do they believe what they believe? What's the emotional story beneath the rational objection?
7.  RANK EVERYTHING: Top 3 pains, top 5 objections, #1 desire. Prioritization helps copywriters know what to lead with.
8.  MAKE IT ACTIONABLE: Don't just say "They're scared of failure." Say: "They're scared of failure. Copy angle: Lead with empathy: 'What if I told you that fear of public failure is the #1 reason events don't sell out?'"
9.  PROVIDE SWIPE FILES: Give ready-to-use hooks, subject lines, and phrases. Copywriters should be able to grab and go.
10. NO FLUFF: Every word must earn its place. Academic language = delete. Vague insights = delete. Anything that doesn't directly help write better copy = delete.
