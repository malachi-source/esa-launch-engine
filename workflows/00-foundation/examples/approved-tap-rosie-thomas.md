## Who We Are Talking To

The Tax and Business Summit attracts a specific buyer. Not a general entrepreneur. Not a hobbyist. A working professional who already has a tax practice, a side hustle, or a service business that produces income but has not yet broken through to the next ceiling. They are stuck between earning and scaling. They show up to rooms because they know proximity is the lever.

Our paid traffic, organic content, SMS, email, and sales scripts must all speak to this person. Everything else is noise.

## Voice and Tone Rules

- Write like a friend who happens to run a real business. Conversational, direct, warm
- No corporate language. No "don't miss this opportunity to network with industry leaders"
- Faith and family references land. Use sparingly and authentically
- Drop names of Dr. Rosie, HIM500, and Alexis Skyy in the first three lines of cold ads
- Always anchor location: Miami. Hyatt Regency. May 22 to 24. People buy the destination as much as the speakers

**Words and Phrases That Convert**

In the room · Proximity · The next level · Real ones, real numbers, real strategies · Bet on yourself · Aligned, called, chosen, purpose · Six figure, seven figure, multiple six figures · Plug, connect, put you on

**Words and Phrases to Avoid**

Synergy, leverage, optimize · Networking opportunity · Industry leaders · Best in class · Take your business to the next level (cliche, kill it) · Don't miss out (low energy, replace with specific scarcity)
