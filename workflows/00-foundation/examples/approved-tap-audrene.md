# TARGET AUDIENCE PROFILE

*Strategic Audience Research and Segmentation*

Prepared for **The Heart of God Retreats LLC** — *Audrene Lopez, Founder*

- June 2026, "The Heart of God" Women's Retreat
- October 2026, "Limitless Heaven" Retreat

Prepared by **Event Sales Agency** — *April 2026*

---

## Executive Summary

This document defines the target audience for The Heart of God Retreats, based on analysis of the client onboarding intake form and the kickoff strategy call conducted on April 15, 2026. It identifies the primary customer avatar, secondary segments, and their core motivations and objections.

The profile is designed to describe who the audience is. It should serve as the foundation for all creative, messaging, and sales conversations across the campaign.

### Key Takeaways

- The primary buyer is a Christian woman, age 45 to 65, spiritually hungry and actively seeking a deeper encounter with God.
- The single most powerful emotional hook is grief and restoration. Audrene's widow testimony creates instant identification with a high-value sub-segment.
- Audience loyalty is built on speaker trust. Ads and nurture sequences should lead with speaker credibility and personal transformation stories.
- The October "Limitless Heaven" event requires an expanded avatar set to include men and couples.

## Psychographics

### Faith Identity

Committed Christian. Most likely identified as Charismatic, Pentecostal, or non-denominational. Open to and actively pursuing supernatural experiences including dreams, visions, prophetic words, and personal encounters with Jesus. Not a cessationist.

### Spiritual Posture

"Hungry." She is not a casual Sunday-only churchgoer. She is actively seeking deeper intimacy with God and tangible breakthrough in areas where she feels stuck. She reads Christian books, attends conferences, and consumes online teaching regularly.

### Core Desires

- A personal, tangible encounter with Jesus
- Healing from grief, trauma, divorce, or loss
- Clarity on her calling and life purpose
- Breakthrough in stagnant areas of her walk with God
- Deeper intimacy with God that moves beyond surface-level faith

### Personality Traits

Reflective. Community-driven. Keeps a prayer journal. Loves worship music. Spends time in intercessory prayer. Emotionally expressive and responsive. Motivated by story and testimony rather than pure logic.

### Media Habits

- **Primary platform.** Facebook, not TikTok. Engages heavily in Christian Facebook groups.
- **Video.** YouTube sermons, worship sessions, and testimony videos.
- **Audio.** Worship music on Spotify or YouTube, and Christian podcasts.
- **Livestreams.** Follows Christian conferences and speakers in real time.

## Pain Points: What Keeps Her Up at Night

*Understanding these pain points is critical to understanding the avatar. She is not buying a "retreat." She is buying the hope of resolving one of these.*

1. **Grief or loss.** The death of a spouse, parent, or child, or the collapse of a marriage. Audrene's widow story resonates most deeply with this group, creating instant emotional identification.
2. **Spiritual plateau.** She has been a Christian for years but feels "stuck." The fire has dimmed and she longs for renewal.
3. **Longing for tangible encounters.** She wants to experience Jesus, not just study about Him. She is tired of head knowledge without heart transformation.
4. **Unclear calling.** She senses God has "more" for her life but cannot articulate what it is or how to step into it.
5. **Loneliness.** Especially among widows and women whose friend circles shifted after major life transitions. She is looking for spiritual sisterhood.
6. **Burdens for family.** She carries unspoken prayers for prodigal children, unbelieving spouses, or hurting family members.

## Objections to Overcome

*Every sales conversation and piece of messaging should be aware of these concerns. Objections that go unaddressed become silent dropoffs.*

- **Cost.** The $260 to $350 ticket plus travel, hotel, and meals. She will calculate the total cost, not just the ticket price.
- **Time away.** From family, work, or caregiving responsibilities. Especially acute for mothers and caregivers.
- **Skepticism.** "Is this legitimate? Is it my kind of Christian." She has been burned by prosperity gospel or manipulative events before.
- **Travel anxiety.** Particularly for older attendees and those traveling alone.
- **Self-doubt.** "What if nothing happens for me. What if God doesn't show up for me like He does for others."

## Secondary Avatar: The Widow in Transition

A high-value sub-segment of the primary avatar that warrants dedicated messaging. Women aged 50 to 75 who have lost a spouse and are in the rebuilding phase of life. Audrene's personal story gives her unmatched credibility and authority with this audience.

### Distinguishing Characteristics

- Often have a life insurance settlement or financial stability that enables discretionary spending
- High willingness to travel for meaningful, transformational experiences
- Strong desire for community with women who genuinely "understand" their loss
- Deep spiritual hunger born directly from grief and the search for meaning
- Often feel invisible in traditional church settings designed around couples and families

### Strategic Importance

This segment is the primary avatar for the future Widows Cruise for 2028 concept mentioned in the intake form. Messaging for this segment should emphasize sisterhood, understanding, and the promise of finding purpose after loss.

## October Event Avatars: Limitless Heaven

The October 30 to November 1 event is a three-day mixed-gender retreat. While the primary avatar remains central, the audience expands to include the following additional segments.

### The Hungry Man, Age 35 to 65

**Profile**

- Spiritually-seeking husband whose wife attended the June retreat and invited him to October
- Single Christian men seeking breakthrough, father-wound healing, and calling clarity
- Divorced or widowed men rebuilding their faith and identity

**Core Messaging Themes**

- Strength, purpose, and masculine identity rooted in Christ
- Father-wound healing and generational breakthrough
- Leadership in family, work, and ministry
- Legacy, and what he leaves behind spiritually

### The Seeking Couple

**Profile**

- Husband and wife pairs wanting a shared spiritual experience away from daily demands
- Couples navigating a difficult season, such as financial strain, parenting challenges, or spiritual disconnect
- Couples in ministry seeking renewal together

**Core Messaging Themes**

- Unity and covenant, growing together spiritually
- Family breakthrough and generational impact
- Restoration of intimacy, with God and with each other
