## SECTION 6: TOP OBJECTIONS & OVERCOMING THEM

### 1. "I'VE NEVER HEARD OF ARENA FOOTBALL / IS THIS REAL?"
**What they're really thinking:**
- Families: "If this is amateur rec league, I'm wasting money and kids will be disappointed"
- Businesses: "If nobody shows up or this isn't legitimate, my brand looks bad"

**Overcome with:**
- Legitimacy markers: "Professional athletes. Official league. Championship trophy. Denver Coliseum. The only thing not major league? The price."
- Social proof: "360+ families grabbed season tickets in 30 days. They're not gambling - they know."
- Pre-emptive reframe: "Arena football = professional football played indoors - same intensity, closer action, way more affordable"

### 2. "WILL PEOPLE ACTUALLY SHOW UP?"
**What they're really thinking:**
- Families: "I don't want to bring kids somewhere embarrassing where we're the only ones there"
- Businesses: "If attendance is weak, my brand exposure is worthless"

**Overcome with:**
- Momentum data: "360+ season ticket leads in 30 days. Targeting 10,000 capacity and on track."
- Track record: Show previous season footage with healthy crowds
- Scarcity: "1,000 season tickets. 360 claimed. Momentum is real."

### 3. "THIS SOUNDS TOO GOOD TO BE TRUE"
**What they're really thinking:**
- Families: "Hidden fees, terrible seats, or fine print that makes this not actually affordable"
- Businesses: "They're lying about results, hiding fees, or this is a scam"

**Overcome with:**
- Transparent pricing: "Season tickets: $250 total. That's it. No hidden fees."
- Economics explanation: "Smaller venues than NFL = lower overhead = lower prices. We pass the savings to fans."
- Show receipts: "Here's the venue contract. Here's previous footage. Here's social impressions data."

### 4. "I CAN'T AFFORD IT RIGHT NOW"
**Overcome with:**
- Value comparison (families): "One Spartans family four-pack ($100) costs less than dinner out - but kids will talk about it for months"
- Payment plans: "$250 total or 5 monthly payments of $50. Same as one streaming subscription."
- ROI framing (businesses): "If 10,000 people see your brand at ONE game and just 3 become customers, did you profit? Now multiply by 5 games."

### 5. "I NEED TO THINK ABOUT IT"
**Overcome with:**
- Urgency without pressure: "360 families decided. Not impulsively - smartly. The difference? They acted while season ticket pricing was available."
- Risk reversal: "Start with ONE game ($35). Prove it. THEN commit to season tickets if you love it."
- Scarcity with deadline: "Season ticket pricing locks January 2026. We're not pressuring - we're trying to save you money."
