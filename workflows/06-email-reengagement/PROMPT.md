Campaign Archetypes for 30–60 Days

Your sequence should pull from these archetypes to keep variety and reactivation strong:

1. Reactivation Opener — "I haven't heard from you in a while…" wake-up message.
2. Big Promise + Future Pace — vivid picture of transformation after the event.
3. Story / Relatable Struggle — share a before/after journey, tie to event solution.
4. Teaching / Quick Win Lesson — give one practical tip/framework they can use right now.
5. Social Proof Spotlight — testimonial or case study story (use names/stats).
6. Myth Buster / Objection Crusher — "you don't need to be an expert… here's why."
7. Behind-the-Scenes / Personal Story — authentic share from founder/speaker.
8. Scarcity Builder — "limited seats, bonuses expire soon."
9. Urgency Push — countdowns, final deadlines, price increases.
10. Real Talk Reset — blunt truth: "Winners do this, others stay stuck."
11. Final Call — cart close, strongest emotional + logical push.

**Structure by Stage (for 30–60 Days)**

**Weeks 1–2 (Reactivation & Value)**

  - Tone: curious, empathetic, "welcome back."
  - Mix: reactivation opener, quick wins, stories, soft CTAs.

**Weeks 3–4 (Authority & Belief Breaking)**

  - Tone: bold, teaching, reframing beliefs.
  - Mix: myth busters, authority builders, social proof spotlights, VIP contrast.

**Weeks 5–6 (Scarcity & Urgency)**

  - Tone: high-energy, urgent, decision-focused.
  - Mix: scarcity builders, bonuses expiring, urgency pushes, final call.

**Guidelines for Each Email**

  - Hook & Story: start with a punchy opener, personal story, or relatable pain.
  - Value First: drop 1 tip, mindset shift, or insight.
  - Future Pace: paint "life after event" vividly.
  - Authority & Proof: use bold results, testimonials, or credibility.
  - CTA: invite them with soft → hard CTA.
  - Formatting: rhythm, spacing, scannable on mobile.

Subject Line Generator (8–20 per email)

Mix urgency, curiosity, and value. Examples:

  - [first_name], are you still stuck here?
  - The one thing schools never told you about [desired outcome]
  - Less than 30 days until [event]
  - 3 steps to [big result]
  - I was wrong about this…
  - Why most people stay broke (and how you won't)
  - Your VIP upgrade is waiting
  - Final chance: [event] closes tonight

**Generation Steps**

1. Read Inputs + Objective/Series Length.

3. Write each email with: Hook → Story → Value → Future Pace → CTA.

5. Ensure tone escalates from warm/value-driven → authority & reframing → scarcity/urgency.
