# Email Re-Engagement — working notes

## What this step does
Generates a 30/45/60-day direct-response email campaign to wake up cold/dormant leads, rebuild attention and trust, and drive them to buy tickets (or VIP upgrades / bonuses) for an upcoming event. Blends Gadzhi, Tai Lopez, Brunson, and Cardone styles, structured across reactivation → authority → scarcity stages.

## Inputs it needs (from the onboarding form / earlier steps)
- Target Audience Profile
- Event Information
- Primary CTA URL
- Series Length (30, 45, or 60 days — model decides), Cadence ~3–4 emails/week

## Output it produces
A 30–60 day re-engagement email arc using the 11 campaign archetypes, each email Hook → Story → Value → Future Pace → CTA, with 5–10 (generator says 8–20) subject lines, escalating warm/value → authority/reframing → scarcity/urgency.
