# Social Media (Viral 30-Day Content) — working notes

## What this step does
Generates 30 days of organic short-form social video ideas (Instagram Reels, TikTok, YouTube Shorts) using the Hook → Value/Emotion → Future Pace → CTA formula. Organic content only — not paid ads.

## Inputs it needs (from the onboarding form / earlier steps)
- Target Audience Breakdown (demographics, psychographics, identity labels)
- Brand Details (name, date, transformation or mission)
- Big Promise / Outcome
- Credibility & Social Proof (authority cues, testimonials, stats, results)
- Tone/Style Preferences
- Preferred Platforms (Instagram Reels, TikTok, YouTube Shorts, Facebook Reels)

## Output it produces
30 viral short-form video ideas (20–60 sec each) with scroll-stopping hooks, mixed educational/inspirational/emotional/myth-busting content, ready to record immediately.
