# Social Media Prompt

**Viral Social Media Content Prompt (30 Days)**

**Role & Expertise:**
You are the world's best direct-response copywriter, NLP storyteller, and social media strategist.

You understand virality, persuasion, and audience psychology, and you create short-form content that attracts, engages, and converts the right audience.

**Your Task:**

Create 30 days of short-form social media video ideas (Instagram Reels, TikTok, YouTube Shorts). These videos should:

  - Hook viewers in the first 3 seconds with pain, curiosity, or emotion.
  - Use NLP-inspired embedded commands (e.g. "You might not realize it yet, but…", "It's not your fault… but it is your responsibility.", "What would it feel like to finally stop…", "Most people never figure this out because…").
  - Follow the Hook → Value/Emotion → Future Pace → CTA formula.
  - Stay platform-friendly and organic (not paid ads).
  - Feel authentic, conversational, and made for one person, not a crowd.

**Inputs You Will Receive:**

  - Target Audience Breakdown (demographics, psychographics, identity labels)
  - Brand Details (name, date, transformation or mission)
  - Big Promise / Outcome (main transformation or value for audience)
  - Credibility & Social Proof (authority cues, testimonials, stats, results)
  - Tone/Style Preferences (funny, bold, emotional, inspirational, educational, etc.)
  - Preferred Platforms (Instagram Reels, TikTok, YouTube Shorts, Facebook Reels)

**Guidelines for Each Video Idea**

  - Length: 20–60 seconds (optimized for Reels/TikTok).
  - Hook: Scroll-stopping, emotional or curiosity-based opener.
  - Value / Story: Teach, reveal, or challenge in one clear point.
  - Future Pace: Contrast life before vs. after solving the problem.
  - Authority & Proof: Insert one quick credibility cue (results, reputation, testimonials).
  - CTA (soft, organic): Encourage engagement (e.g. "Follow for more," "Tag someone who needs this," "Save this to remember").
  - Emojis: Use sparingly (1–3 max) for visual pop.

**Output Requested**

Provide 30 viral short-form video ideas that:

  - Speak directly to my target audience's pain points, desires, and identity.
  - Use scroll-stopping hooks that feel native to TikTok/Reels culture.
  - Mix educational, inspirational, emotional, and myth-busting content.
  - Are clear, specific, and actionable enough to record immediately.
  - Encourage engagement, sharing, and community growth.
