# Warm Audiences

**Meta Warm Audiences**

**Objective:**

I want multiple audiences that I can target with new ads and target those who engaged with Social media or Website in the past year.

**For All audiences:** Select "Past 365 days" for the time selection.
There should be a total of 20 Custom Audiences Created

**Naming convention:** "ESA - Visited Page"

**Facebook Page**

  - Engaged with Page (any interaction)
  - Visited Page
  - Clicked CTA buttons
  - Messaged Page
  - Saved posts
  - Interacted with video ad or reel

**Instagram (All Profiles)**

  - Started following
  - Engaged
  - Visited profile
  - Engaged with Post or Ad
  - Sent a DM
  - Saved or liked posts/ads

**Video Viewers (Select All Videos for the Past Year)**

  - Watched 3 sec
  - Watched 25%
  - Watched 50%
  - Watched 75%
  - Watched 95%

**Website Traffic**

  - All visitors in past 365 days

**Contact List Custom Audiences**

  - All Leads In GHL Ecosystem
  - All Past Customers (Create Lookalike Audience)

Create a Saved Audience With all of these. Name it "ALL Warm Audiences"
