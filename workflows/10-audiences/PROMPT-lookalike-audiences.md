# Lookalike Audiences

**Lookalike Audiences**

**Objective:**

  - Find new Audiences that look like their social media engagers
  - This will help meta find the lookalikes audiences of the lookalikes given.

**Facebook Page**

  - Clicked CTA buttons
  - Messaged Page
  - Interacted with video ad or reel

**Instagram (All Profiles)**

  - Started following
  - Engaged with Post or Ad
  - Sent a DM
  - Saved or liked posts/ads

**Video Viewers**

  - Watched 50%
  - Watched 75%
  - Watched 95%

**Contact List Custom Audiences**

  - All Leads In GHL Ecosystem
  - All Past Customers

Create a Saved Audience With all of these. Name it "All Top Lookalikes"
