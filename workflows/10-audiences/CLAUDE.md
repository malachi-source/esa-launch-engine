# Audiences (Warm + Lookalike) & UTM Tracking — working notes

## What this step does
Sets up Meta audiences for the launch: 20 Warm Custom Audiences (retargeting people who engaged with social/website in the past year, saved as "ALL Warm Audiences"), Lookalike Audiences seeded from top engagers (saved as "All Top Lookalikes"), and the standard UTM tracking code for Meta ad URLs.

## Inputs it needs (from the onboarding form / earlier steps)
- Client's connected Meta assets (Facebook Page, Instagram profiles, video library)
- Website traffic / Pixel installed
- GHL contact list (All Leads, All Past Customers) for Custom Audience upload
- Brand naming convention (e.g. "ESA - Visited Page")

## Output it produces
- 20 Warm Custom Audiences + a saved audience "ALL Warm Audiences" (all set to Past 365 days)
- Lookalike source audiences + a saved audience "All Top Lookalikes"
- UTM tracking string to paste into Meta ad URL parameters
