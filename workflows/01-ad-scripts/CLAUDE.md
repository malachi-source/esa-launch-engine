# 01 Ad Scripts — working notes

## What this step does
Generates 10 unique, high-converting 45–60 second Meta video ad scripts per campaign. Each script follows the structure Pain → Connection → Transformation → CTA, written in spoken voice, modeled on Tai Lopez, Russell Brunson, and Grant Cardone. The prompt lives in `PROMPT.md`; an approved swipe file of 30 real examples lives in `examples/30-example-scripts.md` to anchor tone and format.

## Inputs it needs (from the onboarding form / earlier steps)
- Audience profile (demographics, pain points, desires, objections, identity labels)
- Offer/event details (name, date, location, CTA, transformation, pricing, benefits)

## Output it produces
10 video ad scripts, each 45–60 seconds, spoken-voice, Meta-policy compliant, with varied hooks/angles, approval-ready.
