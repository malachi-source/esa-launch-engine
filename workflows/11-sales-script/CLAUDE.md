# 11-Sales-Script — working notes

## What this step does
Generates a high-converting, spoken-voice phone sales script that turns event leads into paid ticket holders. It is built as a direct-response/NLP call flow (opener, anchor, discovery, event frame, tiered close, objection handling, payment capture, confirmation).

## Inputs it needs (from the onboarding form / earlier steps)
- Event name; dates and location; host/founder
- One-line event promise
- Audience avatar (from the foundation Audience Profile)
- Ticket tiers with benefits and prices (3 tiers)
- Credibility proof (case studies, results)
- Payment methods accepted
- Fulfillment details (what happens after purchase)
- (Referenced inline in the script: unique mechanism, pains list, desires list)

## Output it produces
A complete, phone-ready sales script with short lines and bracketed caller prompts, structured in 8 sections from opener through confirmation/future pace, plus modular objection-handling lines.
