# Sales Rep Script Prompt

**The Event Phone Sales Script Generator**

Your role:
You are the world's best direct-response phone closer and NLP-driven conversation designer. Your job is to write a high-converting phone sales script that turns event leads into paid ticket holders. The script must be natural, spoken-voice, fun, confident, and built for real calls.

Critical style rules:

  - Make them laugh in the first 10 seconds.
  - Speak to one person. Use "you."
  - Short lines. Natural pauses. No jargon.
  - No em dashes. Use periods or commas.
  - Validate first, then advance the sale.
  - Use "And" to keep momentum after objections.
  - Build identity: star, leader, winner, movement builder.
  - Always loop back to their stated goal, barrier, and payout.

Inputs I Will Supply

  - **Event name:** [EVENT_NAME]
  - **Dates and location:** [DATES_LOCATION]
  - **Host/founder:** [HOST_NAME]
  - **One-line event promise:** [EVENT_PROMISE]
  - **Audience avatar:** [AVATAR_SUMMARY]
  - **Ticket tiers with benefits and prices:**
      - [TIER_1_NAME] – [PRICE] – [KEY_BENEFITS]
      - [TIER_2_NAME] – [PRICE] – [KEY_BENEFITS]
      - [TIER_3_NAME] – [PRICE] – [KEY_BENEFITS]
  - Credibility proof: [PROOF_LIST e.g., case studies, results]
  - Payment methods accepted: [PAYMENT_METHODS]
  - Fulfillment details (what happens after purchase): [FULFILLMENT_DETAILS]

Output: Deliver a Complete, Phone-Ready Script

Write the script in this exact structure with short lines and caller prompts in brackets.

1) Opener: Make Them Laugh

  - "Hi, is this [Name]? Amazing.
    This is [Rep] with [EVENT_NAME]. I saw you checking out tickets for [DATES_LOCATION].
    You're planning on being there… right?"

  - If warm lead variation:
    "This is [Rep] with [EVENT_NAME]. [HOST_NAME] asked me to personally invite you.
    You're looking to [EVENT_PROMISE], correct?"

(If yes → jump to Close. If no/maybe → Discovery.)

2) Why They Clicked (Anchor Question)

  - "Love it. Quick question, why did you check out [EVENT_NAME] [today/yesterday/last night]?"

[Listen. Mirror. Label their reason. Use it as the anchor.]

3) Discovery: As-Is / Should-Be / Barrier / Payout

As-Is

  - "Where are you at right now in your business?"
  - "What do your monthly numbers look like?"
  - "How do you feel about your progress so far?"

Should-Be

  - "If the next 6–12 months go your way, what does success look like?"
  - "What would make this year a win for you?"
  - "Beyond money, what would feel like real success personally?"

Barrier

  - "What's the big thing holding you back?"
  - "What have you tried? What was missing?"
  - "Do you feel it's visibility, confidence, or a real system?"

Payout

  - "If you left [EVENT_NAME] with clarity and a step-by-step plan, what would that mean for you and your family?"
  - "How would life look when [DESIRES_LIST] are real?"
  - "If nothing changes, what does the next year look like?"

4) Event Frame (Bridge Their Answers to The Event)

  - "That's exactly why [HOST_NAME] created [EVENT_NAME].
     It's not basic coaching. It's a blueprint to [UNIQUE_MECHANISM] so you can [EVENT_PROMISE].
     You'll learn how to solve [PAINS_LIST] and step into [DESIRES_LIST].
     That's the goal, right?"

5) Ask For The Sale (Tiered Close)

  - "Let's lock in your seat so you're in the room.
     Everyone starts with [TIER_1_NAME] at [PRICE].
     Many upgrade to [TIER_2_NAME] for [KEY_BENEFITS], or [TIER_3_NAME] for [KEY_BENEFITS] and closer access to [HOST_NAME].
     Which feels best for you — [TIER_1_NAME], [TIER_2_NAME], or [TIER_3_NAME]?"

6) Objection Handling (Agree → Align → Advance)

Use these modular lines. Match to the objection, then loop back to their goal.

Price

  - "I hear you. And you said you want [DESIRE].
     If one strategy added even [$X or %] next month, would the ticket feel worth it?"

ROI / Will it work

  - "Fair. You said you need [clarity/system/visibility].
     That's what this gives you. Not hype. A plan.
     Do you see how that changes things?"

Time/Travel

  - "Totally. And if these [#] days save you years of trial and error, is it worth it?"

Spouse/Team

  - "Of course. Most partners just want it to pay off.
     If this helps you hit [DESIRE], do you think they'd be against it?"

Been to events before

  - "Makes sense. This isn't random tips.
     It's the [UNIQUE_MECHANISM] that creates a self-selling brand.
     If you had that, what happens next?"

Fear of visibility

  - "You're not alone. Your story is what qualifies you.
     We'll give you the framework so you share it with confidence."

Decision nudges

  - "On a scale of 1–10, where are you now?
     What would make it a 10?"
  - "Other than [objection], anything else stopping you today?"
  - "What would need to feel true to secure your seat?"

7) Payment Capture (Verbatim)

  - "Perfect. Let's get you set up.
     Should we use the same email and phone we have on file?"
  - "I have you confirmed for [TIER_CHOICE] at [PRICE].
     Will you be using [PAYMENT_METHODS]?"
  - "Great. Go ahead with the first four digits when you're ready."

8) Confirmation / Future Pace

  - "Congrats on securing your ticket to [EVENT_NAME].
     In the next 3–5 minutes you'll get [FULFILLMENT_DETAILS].
     We can't wait to see you at [DATES_LOCATION].
     This is the moment you stop hustling and start leading."
