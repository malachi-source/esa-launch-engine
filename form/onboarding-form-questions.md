## What changed in this merge (v3)
- **Kept all the operational depth** from your live form: target attendees, first-event, reps/team, sales process, tracking, payment processor + data flow, A2P 10DLC, sending domain, old-lead segmentation, brand/competitor snapshot, final approver. This is what makes the GHL build + launch executable.
- **Upgraded the audience section** to your 10 keystone questions (Section 4). These REPLACE the old thin audience questions ("Who is your ideal customer?", "number one thing clients want", "single most competitive advantage", "painful life situation"). The verbatim-quotes question (Q25), the avoid-list (Q28), and the not-a-fit (Q29) are brand-new and are the biggest copy-quality upgrade.
- **Added a few essentials the old form lacked:** event type, event time+timezone, curriculum, transformation, GHL/Meta/Pixel status, what's-included per tier, guarantees/scarcity.
