# ESA Client Onboarding Form — Questions (v5 — lean, 31 questions)

> Goal of every question: directly help us FILL the webinar with IDEAL attendees who CONVERT
> into the client's high-ticket offer. No fluff. Sourced from the existing esabuilder form +
> Malachi's 10 keystone questions, then condensed and merged. Competitor questions removed
> (we research competitors ourselves).
>
> Type = answer format. Req = ✅ required. Feeds = which prompt uses it
> (PS=Project Summary, ES=Event Summary, AP=Audience Profile, OFFER=VSL/Sales/Ad copy, TECH=build/launch).

## Required hard-stops
Contact (1–4), plus the keystone hard-stops **Q13, Q15, Q16, Q19, Q20** (Q16 matters most),
plus the offer (Q11, Q12), goal (Q9), and ad budget (Q26).

## What was cut from the original 68 (and why)
- **Competitor websites + competitor likes/avoid** — removed; we research competitors ourselves.
- **Duplicates the keystone 10 already cover better:** "Who is your ideal customer?", the whole "Marketing Questionnaire" (#1 thing clients want, what you do, products/standout), "single competitive advantage."
- **Low-impact / pure-ops:** is-this-your-first-event, team member list, internal marketing person, lead qualification, lead contact speed, speakers promoting, ad-creatives-folder, static-vs-video, current SMS/email strategy, painful-life-situation story, final approver.
- **Merged into one question each:** payment + data flow (3→1), deliverability setup (3→1), old leads (3→1), brand assets (6→1), current status (3→1), outbound/reps (2→1).

Result: **31 focused questions**, every one serving fill → attract ideal → convert to high-ticket.
