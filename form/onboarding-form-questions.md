## 13) Audience Deep-Dive — Malachi's 10 keystone questions
> Verbatim wording. Helper text (italic) shows on the live form. Q62 (real customer quotes) carries the most weight.

| # | Question | Type | Req | Feeds |
|---|----------|------|-----|-------|
| 59 | Who is the ideal person who should buy a ticket? *(The avatar. Be specific, not "everyone.")* | Long text | ✅ | AP |
| 60 | Age range, gender split, and where they live? *(Hard demographics.)* | Short text | ⬜ | AP |
| 61 | What is the #1 problem they're struggling with right now? *(The pain everything leads with.)* | Long text | ✅ | AP |
| 62 | Paste 5–10 real things your customers have actually said. *(Exact words, slang, typos left in — becomes the ad copy.)* | Long text | ✅ | AP |
| 63 | What do they really want underneath that, and who do they want to become? *(Identity-level desire.)* | Long text | ⬜ | AP |
| 64 | Top reasons they hesitate or don't buy, and the real fear under each. *(Objections, decoded.)* | Long text | ⬜ | AP |
| 65 | What words, phrases, or vibes instantly turn them off or feel fake? *(The avoid-list.)* | Long text | ✅ | AP |
| 66 | Who is NOT a good fit / who don't you want in the room? *(Protects ad spend + call quality.)* | Long text | ✅ | AP |
| 67 | Do different types of people buy different ticket tiers? Describe each. | Long text | ⬜ | AP |
| 68 | In one sentence: why do customers choose YOU, and the one transformation you deliver? | Long text | ⬜ | AP, OFFER |
